💡 Pro Tips

MedicationsAlways pack prescriptions in your carry-on bag — never in checked luggage.
AllergiesLife-threatening allergy? Keep your EpiPen and Benadryl accessible at all times.
Gut healthStart probiotics a week before departure. Add activated charcoal as backup.
Plug adapterCheck your destination's plug type ahead of time and pick up the right adapter before you go.
Light packingBring laundry detergent sheets — hand wash and rewear to keep your bag light.
