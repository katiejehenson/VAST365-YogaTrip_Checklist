# ✈️ Group Travel Checklist
**Pack smart. Travel light. Arrive ready.**

</div>

---

## 📋 Documents & Bookings

- [ ] **Passport** — valid 6+ months beyond your travel dates *(check expiration date NOW)*
- [ ] **Visa requirements** for your destination — [🔗 US Citizens: State Dept Visa Info](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/us-visas.html)
- [ ] **Air tickets** — printed or downloaded offline
- [ ] **Train / ferry tickets** *(if applicable)* — screenshot confirmations in case of no wifi
- [ ] **Travel insurance** purchased & docs saved — [🔗 State Dept Insurance Guide](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/international-travel/before-you-go/your-health-abroad/Insurance_Coverage_Overseas.html)
- [ ] **Photocopies of passport & key docs** — store separately from originals + email to yourself
- [ ] **Emergency contacts list** written down

---

## 👗 Clothing & Accessories

- [ ] **Yoga clothes & swimsuit**
- [ ] **Laundry detergent** for hand washing *(lighten your luggage — rewear & rewash!)*
- [ ] **Comfy clothes** for lounging or exploring
- [ ] **Long sleeved lightweight shirt & sarong** *(temples, sun protection & cool evenings)*
- [ ] **Good walking shoes**
- [ ] **Flip flops and/or sandals**
- [ ] **Socks & underwear** *(don't forget!)*
- [ ] **Sunglasses & hat(s)**
- [ ] **Extra duffle bag** for shopping goodies

---

## 💊 Health & Toiletries

- [ ] **Toiletries & feminine products**
- [ ] **Medications** packed in carry-on ⚠️ *(always keep prescriptions with you)*
- [ ] **EpiPen & Benadryl** *(if you have life-threatening allergies — never check these)*
- [ ] **Probiotics** for digestive health *(start taking a week before you leave!)*
- [ ] **Activated charcoal** for unexpected tummy troubles
- [ ] **Sunscreen** *(reef-safe preferred)*

---

## 🎒 Gear & Essentials

- [ ] **Reusable water bottle**
- [ ] **Plug adapter** — Greece uses Type C & F *(two round parallel pins — pick up before you go)*
- [ ] **All charging cables** *(phone, laptop, headphones, camera...)*
- [ ] **Ear plugs** *(for light sleepers)*
- [ ] **Journal & pen**
- [ ] **Good book to read**

## 💡 Pro Tips

> **Medications:** Always pack prescriptions in your carry-on bag — never in checked luggage.

> **Allergies:** Life-threatening allergy? Pack your EpiPen and Benadryl and keep them accessible at all times.

> **Gut health:** Start taking probiotics a week before departure. Add activated charcoal as backup.

> **Greece plugs:** Type C & F (two round parallel pins). Pick up a universal adapter before you go.

> **Light packing:** Bring laundry detergent sheets — hand wash and rewear to keep your bag light.
